We build classrooms without ceilings.

At The World Public School, Dabra, we believe education is more than marks on a report card — it's the confidence to ask questions, the discipline to pursue mastery, and the empathy to lead with kindness. Every teacher on our campus is a mentor first, and every child is known by name, not roll number.
